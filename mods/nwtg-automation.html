Since the last news post I have been working hard on getting the website to look a bit better, major changes have been made to the CSS script for the site. The site now should be more mobile friendly and easier to read. This was no small task to implment, I still have the wiki to work on as I did start a few pages for it. However, now that most of the site is set up, and now that there is a dedicated downloads page it will be fine to coninue with the development.

There are still things I would like to work on but the most important part was having the downloads, and main mod pages set up and viewable for everyone. This upcoming week I will be working mainly on the modification and wiki pages, I will try to spread out the time on both projects. But for now I am taking the rest of the weekend off to relax and wind down.

RECIPES NOW 79% DONE

POSTED: AUG 20th, 2021

Just a quick update, at the moment recipes are 79% complete. Armor has a known issues using the new tag system and has been disabled for now. I tested 2021.2 to see if the issue was in that MCreator version and its not, So tag support should be coming after MCreator 2021.2 is released. There are just a few recipes still needing to be worked on but I need to finish adding the devices, and adding support for the new padlock.

The next two or so days will be focused on finishing the reworking of the right-click procedures for the devices, the script is made, all I need to do is addapt it to the the exisiting blocks and test them again. This should take over the weekend to do but will be worth it in the end as from testing its much more enjoyable with the new sounds.

ITEMS COMPLETE 0.3.0

POSTED: AUG 18th, 2021

Currently progress is going well, many things have moved to the testing phase, and all the items are now added and done. Blocks are not to far to follow. After which I will be focusing on getting the reworking done for the block already added in the last two update. May recipes are still needing testing, I think I may have tested them but I don't remember doing it so I will be testing them again just to be safe. Also one thing still neededing to do for crafting/smelting recipes is make them unlocable, this will need to be done before testing.

I have also added many more auto crafting recipes to the list of things to add, some recipes are not possible to fully automate so I have not bothered to add them until they can be automated, it saves work this way. Regarding the other devices, they will be added in good time, I still have to finish the padlock, its mostly done, I have some minor stuff to do like sound effects and such but, other than that its been tested with the auto fishing machine and seems to work properly.

On that note, it may be a couple more weeks until the release, I am not going to know when until I am on the final stages of testing which by then it will be quickly published after testing is done. Any bugfixes will be release aftwords.

NWTG AUTOMATION 0.3.0 PROGRESS
