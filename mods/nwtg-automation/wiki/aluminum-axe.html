Wiki - Aluminum Axe

Overview

The Aluminum Axe is a tool that can be used to break most wood blocks. The axe has the similar properties as an Iron Axe. The axe can also be used to craft Auto Tree Farm Machines as an alternitive crafting resource.

Recipes

The axe can be crafted at a Crafting Table with a similar shape as axe recipes in vanilla.
