Wiki - Upgrades

Overview

Upgrades where added in version 0.3.0. They are used to make machines have bonus perks like improved speed or yield. There are six tiers of upgrades (wooden, stone, iron, golden, diamond, and netherite). As of right now they are only used with the Auto Farming Machine but support will be added to other machines in the future.
