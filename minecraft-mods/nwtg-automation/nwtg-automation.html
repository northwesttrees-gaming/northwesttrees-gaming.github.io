NWTG AUTOMATION 0.3.0 Progress

POSTED: AUG 11th, 2021

Development is coming along really well, The Auto Fishing Machine is just about done. There is some work still needed to be done on the Padlock block, however, I am happy with the progress I have made over the last few days with the mod.

One of the things I am thinking about doing is tutorials for each machine on how to use them, how to craft them etc. This will save time for updating the wiki pages for the modification and will give people a better idea how things work with an in-game example.
